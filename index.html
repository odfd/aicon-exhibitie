Art Exhibition focused on AI

AI is a rapidly evolving technology that only just recently is starting to impact society as a whole. This technology will change society as we know it. AICON aims to embrace this technology and make it conceptually accessible to all! To reach this goal AICON is designing thought provoking art expositions that are co-created using cutting edge AI technology with the inputs of inhabitants of Rotterdam, artists and scientists.

Co-created AI Art (room 1)

High quality art pieces , all art and captions are generated by AI - without mentioning it was created by AI! By showing visitors that AI-generated art can be aesthetically pleasing and thought-provoking, and presenting a diverse range of styles, including both abstract and highly detailed pieces (optional), this will create a sense of surprise and wonder when visitors discover that the art was created by a machine. Passive audio recording images mosaic projected on wall with physical frame borders

Meet the Artist!

(Passive audio recordings) show how mosaics are made: show text recordings used, images generated, selection of the generated images. Visually demonstrate the ‘behind the scenes’ process, make it seem like the ‘AI artist’ is constantly recording audio, speech to text, processing the text, extracting keywords, prompt engineering, generating images/art, selecting from the generated images and ‘sending’ to the mosaic (the mosaic is projected in room 1)

Educational story diagrams (wall-sized posters) presented with an artistic style, about 3-4 AI topics, relevant to both the technology used in the exhibition, and relevant to the visitors. These diagrams will address thought provoking and sometimes controversial aspects of the selected topics, and shine light on the positive ways in which AI can be used to benefit each of us/the visitors as individuals, and society as a whole.
